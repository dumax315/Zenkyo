Mental health cannot be solved with a one-size-fits-all approach.
We gathered a wide variety of different ideas to suit the communicative needs of victims based on their personal experiences—ZENKYO.

Web-App Evidence Collection

All types of abuse or other difficulties in life frequently require efficient organization and proof. This is necessary in order to build a strong case that can help a victim receive the dignity, justice, and respect they deserve. Zenkyo offers a secure database for anyone to upload and organize their own evidence and testimonials.

We also offer a self-quiz for victims as well as one for abusers to recognize their communicative problems.
